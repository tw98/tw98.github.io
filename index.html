HELLO, I'M TOM!

I am a recent graduate from Yale College with a major in Computer Science.

In addition to my studies, I conduct research under the guidance of Brian Scassellati, Smita Krishnaswamy, and Marynel Vázquez.

My main interests center around reinforcement learning and human-robot interaction. In my future work, I would love to contribute to the development of autonomous intelligent agents that can cooperate with people and other agents in diverse environments.

I am also interested in applying machine learning methods to help find solutions benefitting the environment and social good.

In my free time, I love to play soccer, go running, and spend time outside.
